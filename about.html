About Me

# My Story – Mwaka Stephen

My name is **Mwaka Stephen**, and my journey is 
one of resilience, determination, and a passion for using technology 
to transform lives.

I grew up facing many challenges, but I learned 

early that education is one of the most powerful tools for creating a 
better future. Living in the **Kyaka II Refugee Settlement 
in Uganda** taught me the value of perseverance, adaptability, and hope.
 Rather than allowing difficult circumstances to define my future, 
 I chose to let them inspire me to dream bigger and work harder.

   My academic journey began at **Kabarole Adventist
     Secondary School**, where I completed my Uganda Certificate
  of Education (UCE) in 2020. During my time there, 
  I demonstrated leadership by serving as **Head Prefect** from 2018 to 2019,
   earning a Certificate of Merit for my service. I later joined **Bankhill College**,
    where I completed my Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) in 2024, 
    studying Physics,Mathematics, Entrepreneurship, and ICT. In 2023,
     
     I also served as the **School Speaker**, further strengthening
      my communication and leadership skills.

    Driven by a deep interest in technology and innovation, 
    I pursued higher education at **ISBAT University**, where I earned a 
    **Bachelor's Degree in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning**.
      At ISBAT University, I gained a strong foundation in machine learning, data science,
  computer vision, natural language processing, intelligent systems,
   software development, and AI ethics. My studies equipped me 
   with the knowledge and practical skills needed to develop
    intelligent solutions capable of solving complex real-world problems.

     Alongside my formal education, I completed computer
   training through **YUFAT** in Kyaka II Refugee
  Settlement and dedicated myself to learning 
  programming through self-study. I also volunteered 
  for one year as an **IT Volunteer** at YUFAT, assisting 
  computer users, troubleshooting technical issues, and 
  supporting digital literacy initiatives within the refugee
   community. This experience reinforced my belief that 
   technology can empower people and bridge opportunities
   , regardless of their background.

Throughout my journey, I have continued to build 
strong technical and personal skills. I am proficient in Microsoft 
Office applications, 
computer troubleshooting, and programming, while also developing
 qualities such as leadership,
 teamwork, communication, resilience, and problem-solving.
  These skills have enabled me to work effectively with diverse teams 
  and remain focused on achieving meaningful goals.

Beyond academics and technology, I enjoy reading, 
writing, learning new skills, and playing football.
 These activities have helped me develop 
 discipline, creativity, and a balanced perspective on life.

Today, my vision is clear: I aspire to become an 
Artificial Intelligence professional who develops innovative 
technologies that improve healthcare, 
education, agriculture, and humanitarian services, 
particularly for displaced and underserved communities. 
I believe AI has the power to solve some of society's greatest challenges when 

applied ethically and responsibly.

My story is proof that circumstances do not 
determine destiny. Through resilience, continuous learning, and
 unwavering determination, I have transformed challenges into opportunities.
  As I continue my professional journey, I remain committed to using 
  Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to create solutions 
  that make a lasting positive impact on communities around the world.
